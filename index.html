About

Quick Access

Quickly access and supply medics with your medical history and other useful information to help save you.

How does it work?

Wings of Health is an app that provides quick and easy way to provide medical information to save a victim. It can help in a variety of situations when medical information is needed. It also provides hospital a single source to find out information on emergency ER patients. Join today, save a life!

Efficient

Wings of Health guarantees quick and responsive help in any situation. The health cards allows for quick and easy access to all useful information. Information will be limited to protect sensitive information.

Health Card

You will be provided with a secure card with a unique ID to quickly access information. This card can be scanned in order to access the data via a secure channel. No sensitive information can be obtained via Health Card to prevent potential breaches.

Security

Wings of Health guarantees privacy of sensitive information, with different levels of security.
